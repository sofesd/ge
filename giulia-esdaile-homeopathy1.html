About Giulia

Giulia Esdaile

BSc (Hons) LicACH, RSHom. I studied conventional medicines at King's College London and trained in classical homeopathy at the LCCH and ACH.

In 2004 I founded my practice in North London, offering personalised homeopathic care with a focus on the whole person. I am a registered member of the Society of Homeopaths and work with both acute and chronic presentations.
